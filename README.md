The Slop Report

The Slop Report is a daily digest tracking AI-generated content, deepfakes, synthetic media, and the broader battle over information quality on the internet.

Every day there's more of it — SEO spam, AI-generated news, synthetic images and audio, low-effort content flooding every corner of the web. The Slop Report cuts through the noise and collects the stories that matter.
What We Cover

    AI-generated content and its impact on media
    Deepfakes and synthetic media
    Platform policies on AI content
    Detection tools and techniques
    Regulatory and legal developments

About This Site

The Slop Report is published daily and aggregated weekly. Posts are generated automatically using Claude AI and curated from reputable tech and media sources.
