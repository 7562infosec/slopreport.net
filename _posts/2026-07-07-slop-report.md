### 1. [Our interest in AI slop is hitting a ceiling](https://www.fastcompany.com/91568818/our-interest-in-ai-slop-is-hitting-a-ceiling?partner=rss&utm_source=rss&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=rss+fastcompany&utm_content=rss)
*Fast Company Tech*  - · Jul 6

Interest in AI-generated content, referred to as "AI slop," appears to be plateauing, despite it
constituting 44% of uploads on some platforms; only 1% to 3% of streams are attributed to such

from discovery issues in recommendation systems rather than a rejection of the content itself,
highlighting the importance of human curation in navigating the growing noise of low-quality AI
outputs in the creative economy.

### 11. [An AI agent just ran a full ransomware attack with no human at the keyboard](https://thenextweb.com/news/jadepuffer-agentic-ai-ransomware)

among governments about the potential for future AI-driven cyber threats.
