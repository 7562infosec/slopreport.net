### 3. [AI spam filters are getting suckered by old-school text salting](https://www.theregister.com/security/2026/07/17/ai-spam-filters-are-getting-suckered-by-old-school-text-salting/5274434)
*The Register*  - · Jul 17

Cybersecurity firm Barracuda has reported that attackers are successfully using an old technique
called "text salting" to bypass AI-powered email filters, resulting in over a million phishing
attempts since April. This method involves embedding random, harmless-looking words within malicious
emails to trick machine learning systems into misclassifying the messages as safe. The challenge
highlights the limitations of current AI email security tools, which struggle to detect hidden
malicious content, prompting experts to recommend a more layered approach to email security rather
than relying solely on AI filters.
