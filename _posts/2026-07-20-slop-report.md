repositories, the study highlights the need for effective remediation strategies to sustain the
health of OSS communities, pointing out that current defensive measures may hinder long-term
openness and sustainability. This research matters as it underscores the challenges posed by
generative AI on collaborative software development, affecting community dynamics and project
viability.
