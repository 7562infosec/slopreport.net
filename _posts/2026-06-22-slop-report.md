### 4. [Value for Money Is All You Need](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48628090)
*Hacker News*  - · Jun 22

The article discusses the evolving landscape of token consumption in artificial intelligence,
highlighting the phenomenon of "TokenMaxxing," where businesses like Uber overextended their budgets
by maximizing the use of expensive proprietary language models (LLMs). In response to the financial
burden, companies are increasingly opting for lower-cost Chinese open-source LLMs, prompting OpenAI
CEO Sam Altman to propose reducing token costs to maintain competitiveness. Ultimately, the article
emphasizes a new approach called "Value for Money," which balances cost, quality, and risk, as the
key criterion for businesses navigating the AI market.

### 11. [Show HN: Pure Effect – Reproduce production bugs on your laptop without a DB](https://pure-effect.org)
*Hacker News*  - · Jun 21

Pure Effect is a newly introduced zero-dependency effect library for JavaScript and TypeScript
designed to separate business logic from I/O operations, allowing developers to reproduce production
bugs on their local machines without requiring a live database. By returning plain objects
representing I/O actions instead of executing them immediately, Pure Effect enables more manageable
testing and debugging, making it easier to identify issues that arise in production. This
advancement is significant for improving software reliability and simplifying the debugging process.
