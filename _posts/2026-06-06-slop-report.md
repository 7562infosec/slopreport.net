### 10. [Show HN: Busbar – every LLM behind one URL, in a single Rust binary](https://github.com/MattJackson/busbarAI)
*Hacker News*  - · Jun 5

distributing traffic across multiple LLM providers, and simplifies the complexity of managing
multiple AI model APIs.
